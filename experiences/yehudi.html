Company Background

Yehudi HQ is an educational non-profit organization dedicated to providing comprehensive educational programs and resources to thousands of students. The organization focuses on delivering high-quality educational experiences while maintaining detailed tracking of student progress and program effectiveness.

Working directly with the COO and CEO, I joined Yehudi HQ at a crucial point in their growth, when they needed to transform their data management capabilities to support their expanding educational programs and student base. My role was instrumental in creating foundational systems that would later facilitate their transition to a Salesforce-based infrastructure.

Main Project: Educational Data Management System

Project Goal

Transform the organization's data management from ground zero to a comprehensive system capable of handling complex educational data while preparing for future Salesforce integration.

Technical Implementation

  • Developed custom Excel scripts for automated weekly reporting

Challenges & Solutions

  • Challenge: Complex database integration requirements
    Solution: Developed flexible system architecture that could interface with Olami database and prepare for Salesforce transition
